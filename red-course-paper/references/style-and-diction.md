# Style And Diction

These resources were extracted from the user's seven image references. Treat them as a diction bank and rhythm guide, not as a mechanical synonym table.

Use only after the argument is already sound. Prefer one or two apt phrases per paragraph. If several ornate phrases appear in one sentence, simplify.

## General Rules

- Replace weak words only when the replacement fits the concept.
- Prefer precise academic terms over decorative terms when discussing theory.
- Use literary terms most safely in introductions, transitions, and conclusions.
- Avoid phrases that sound like motivational posters unless the assignment is reflective or youth-themed.
- If a phrase is too intense for a normal paper sentence, convert it into plainer academic prose.

## Transition Replacements

Use these to vary paragraph logic. Do not overuse them.

### Concession And Acknowledgment

- 虽然 -> 尽管如此、即使如此、纵然如是
- 当然 -> 毋庸讳言、固然、诚然
- 退一步说 -> 即便退一步讲、纵使我们承认

### Contrast And Turn

- 但是 -> 问题的症结在于、耐人寻味的是、吊诡的是
- 与此不同 -> 与之形成鲜明对照的是、反观、反视
- 反而 -> 非但没有……反而、适得其反、事与愿违

### Cause And Inference

- 因为 -> 根植于、导源于、滥觞于
- 导致 -> 诱发、引致、致使、酿成
- 所以 -> 鉴于此、有鉴于此、基于此、由此观之

### Summary And Conclusion

- 总之 -> 一言以蔽之、概而言之、归根结底
- 最终 -> 终而、归根到底、归于
- 可以说 -> 无疑是、堪称、实乃

## Cautions

Some expressions are vivid but risky in academic prose:

- "星辰大海", "逆风翻盘", "破茧成蝶", "向光而行" can sound like motivational writing. Use only in youth-themed reflections or conclusions.
- "苍生", "魂魄", "碧血丹心" are highly literary. Use sparingly.
- "硬核", "卡点", "风口" are colloquial-policy style. They may fit innovation essays but not formal theoretical paragraphs.
- "滥觞于" means "originates from"; do not use it merely as "because".
- "酿成" usually implies a negative consequence.
