# Negative Prompts

Use this file before drafting and again during revision. These are not banned words in every context; they are warning signs. If a phrase appears, ask whether it adds analysis. If not, replace it with a concrete claim, mechanism, source, or example.

## Core Rule

Do not make a red-course paper sound correct by piling up formulaic language. The paper should show:

- What question is being answered.
- Which course concept explains it.
- What historical, theoretical, policy, or personal material supports it.
- Through what mechanism the conclusion follows.

If a sentence cannot answer at least one of these, cut or rewrite it.

## 1. Overused Contrast Frames

Avoid high-frequency use of:

- 不是……而是……
- 不仅是……更是……
- 既不是……也不是……而是……
- 与其说……不如说……
- 从……到……, 从……到……

These frames are useful once or twice, especially for a thesis. Repeating them makes the paper sound like generated commentary.

Positive examples must not reuse the same contrast frames they are replacing.

Prefer:

- Direct definition: "这里讨论的资本，是一种历史性的社会生产关系。"
- Mechanism: "问题发生在生产社会化与私人占有之间的张力之中。"
- Scope correction: "这一判断不能停留在道德评价层面，还要回到历史条件。"
- Paragraph-level contrast instead of sentence-level formula: make one paragraph present the common view, then the next paragraph correct it.

Quick check: if three adjacent paragraphs all use contrast frames, rewrite at least two.

## 2. Empty Policy And Management Buzzwords

Use these carefully:

- 闭环
- 赋能
- 核心
- 锚定
- 抓手
- 底座
- 路径
- 场域
- 矩阵
- 生态
- 协同
- 机制
- 全链条
- 高质量
- 系统性
- 整体性

These words are acceptable when they name an official concept or a real analytical relation. They become weak when used as decoration.

Weak:

> 以制度闭环赋能青年成长，锚定高质量发展核心路径。

Better:

> 制度的作用在于把价值要求转化为稳定规则，使个人选择、公共责任和评价标准之间形成可预期的关系。

Rule: after using one of these words, name the actor, institution, condition, and result. If that cannot be done, replace the word.

## 3. Abrupt Or Overheated Metaphors

Avoid emotionally colored metaphors in serious theoretical prose unless the surrounding style supports them:

- 牛鼻子
- 硬骨头
- 压舱石
- 定盘星
- 组合拳
- 突破口
- 主引擎
- 加速器
- 生命线
- 金钥匙
- 蓄水池
- 晴雨表
- 试金石
- 施工图
- 新赛道

Some of these are common in official commentary. In student papers, especially Marxist theory or modern history essays, they can sound abrupt if inserted into concept analysis.

Prefer precise theoretical language:

- "关键矛盾" instead of "牛鼻子"
- "难点问题" instead of "硬骨头"
- "稳定条件" instead of "压舱石"
- "判断标准" instead of "试金石"
- "制度安排" instead of "施工图"

Use metaphor only when it clarifies, not when it tries to create momentum.

## 4. Slogan-Like Elevation Without Argument

Avoid ending paragraphs with unsupported elevation:

- 彰显时代价值
- 具有重要意义
- 提供深刻启示
- 贡献青春力量
- 书写时代答卷
- 奔赴星辰大海
- 在时代浪潮中勇立潮头
- 为民族复兴注入强劲动力

These may fit a short speech or youth reflection, but they weaken course papers when not earned by analysis.

Replace with:

- A smaller conclusion from the paragraph.
- A practical implication.
- A specific institutional, historical, or conceptual consequence.

Weak:

> 这为新时代青年提供了深刻启示。

Better:

> 对青年而言，这一判断把个人发展重新放回现实条件之中：能力形成于社会实践，责任也要在具体岗位和公共生活中落实。

## 5. Model-Like Summary Catchphrases

Avoid using chat-style summary signals inside the paper:

- 一句话说
- 一句话总结
- 简单来说
- 简而言之
- 总的来说
- 总而言之
- 概括起来说
- 归根结底

These phrases can be useful in oral explanation, but in academic prose they often reveal a model-like habit of closing a section with a packaged conclusion. They also flatten argument: the paragraph sounds as if it is reporting a summary rather than developing a claim.

Prefer:

- Let the final sentence state the exact consequence of the preceding analysis.
- Use the course concept itself to close the paragraph.
- Move the summary work into the paragraph topic sentence instead of announcing it.

Weak:

> 一句话总结，青年应该在时代发展中贡献自己的力量。

Better:

> 青年的责任不能停留在态度表明上，还要落实为对专业学习、公共生活和社会实践的持续参与。

Rule: if "一句话说" or similar phrases appear, delete the signal phrase first. Then check whether the remaining sentence still has a real claim. If not, rewrite it around a concept, mechanism, or concrete implication.

## 6. Vague Attribution

Avoid:

- 专家指出
- 相关研究表明
- 历史证明
- 事实说明
- 有观点认为
- 社会普遍认为
- 资料显示

Use them only with a named source, document, author, textbook, case, or data point.

Better:

- "《马克思主义基本原理》教材在阐述生产力与生产关系时强调……"
- "《中华人民共和国民营经济促进法》把促进发展与规范经营放在同一法律框架中。"
- "《共产党宣言》对资产阶级历史作用的评价，说明马克思并未把资本简单理解为道德对象。"

## 7. Mechanical Three-Part Completeness

Watch for unnecessary triads:

- 理论上、实践上、价值上
- 历史逻辑、理论逻辑、现实逻辑
- 认识问题、分析问题、解决问题
- 个人、社会、国家
- 过去、现在、未来

Three-part structures are not wrong. The problem is forcing every paragraph into three parts to sound comprehensive.

Prefer two real tensions when they fit the topic:

- 效率与公平
- 活力与秩序
- 发展与规范
- 自由与规则
- 生产力与生产关系
- 历史进步与历史代价

Rule: if the third item only repeats the first two, remove it.

## 8. Concept Piling

Avoid stacking course concepts without explanation:

> 在历史唯物主义、辩证唯物主义、生产力与生产关系、经济基础与上层建筑的统一中，体现了科学社会主义的时代价值。

Better:

> 这一问题首先可以从生产力与生产关系的矛盾理解：当生产力发展到一定阶段，原有生产关系既可能推动它，也可能限制它。

Use one concept at a time. Explain it in the writer's own words before moving to the next concept.

## 9. Ornamental Humanization

Do not add "human flavor" by inserting random personal feeling, campus scenes, or literary sentences into a theory-heavy paper.

Avoid:

- Sudden youth-emotion endings in a Marxist theory paper.
- Personal anecdotes that do not connect to the course concept.
- Decorative idioms inserted only to make the text "not AI."
- Replacing precise theory terms with florid expressions.

Good humanization means clearer judgment, varied sentence rhythm, and real specificity. It does not mean making every paragraph lyrical.

## 10. Current-Affairs Name Dropping

Avoid listing recent policies only to look current:

> 二十大、二十届三中全会、二十届四中全会、十五五规划、政府工作报告共同说明……

Better:

> 以“十五五”规划关于高水平社会主义市场经济体制的部署为例，市场作用、主体活力和社会主义基本经济制度被放在同一框架中理解，资本运行的空间与边界也由此获得制度说明。

Use at most two current examples in a normal paper. Explain one well rather than naming five.

## 11. Revision Checklist

Before delivery, scan for:

- More than two "不是……而是……" frames in one section.
- Buzzwords without named mechanism.
- Metaphors that clash with academic tone.
- Paragraph endings that only elevate.
- Chat-style summary signals such as "一句话说" or "一句话总结".
- Vague authorities without source.
- Three-part lists that feel automatic.
- Theory concepts stacked without explanation.

When in doubt, rewrite toward plain mechanism: who acts, under what condition, through what institution or relation, producing what result.
