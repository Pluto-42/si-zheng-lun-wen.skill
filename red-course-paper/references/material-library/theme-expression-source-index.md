### 作文素材丨人民日报等央媒评论中常用的高级词、固定搭配

- URL: https://www.sohu.com/a/440608381_271046
- Type: expression bank from 2020.
- Observed organization: "plain phrase -> media-style collocation -> example source" format.
- Best use: convert plain essay verbs into restrained official-commentary collocations.
- Red-course adaptation: keep pattern categories rather than copying fixed wording:
  - action verb + concrete base: e.g. build foundation, strengthen bond, activate momentum.
  - concept + mechanism: e.g. explain "why it works", not just "it is important".
  - problem phrase + institutional diagnosis: name the pain point, then locate the system relation.
- Risk: this resource is from 2020 and may contain contexts that are stale. Treat it as diction pattern, not as current factual material.

## Ingestion Rule

For every theme-material source, store only:

1. URL and date.
2. Topic categories.
3. Usable organization pattern.
4. Time-sensitive items to remove or refresh.
5. A short adaptation note for red-course papers.

Do not store full passages from素材站. Their value is in classification and packaging, not in direct reuse.
