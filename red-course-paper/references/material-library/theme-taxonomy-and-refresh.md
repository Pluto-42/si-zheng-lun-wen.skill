# Theme Taxonomy And Refresh Rules

Synced on 2026-05-31. This file converts exam-oriented作文主题 into red-course paper themes, and replaces stale examples with current 2025/2026 materials.

## Stable Theme Taxonomy

### 1. 家国情怀 / 国家认同

Use for: 思想道德与法治, 近代史纲要, youth responsibility, national rejuvenation.

Core question: how does personal life connect with the fate of the country and the people?

Current examples:

- 2026 "十五五"规划纲要 as the new national development blueprint for 2026-2030.
- 2026 government work report as annual governance and development task summary.
- Youth participation in Chinese modernization, rural revitalization, technology, culture, and public service.

Avoid:

- Generic "强国有我" ending without concrete action.
- Treating patriotism as emotion only; connect it to responsibility, capability, and practice.

### 2. 时代青年 / 责任担当

Use for: 思想道德与法治, 中国特色社会主义, youth-themed assignments.

Core question: what should young people do under the current stage of Chinese modernization?

Current examples:

- Education Power Plan 2024-2035: links education, talent, technology, and "时代新人".
- 2026 "十五五"规划: gives youth-oriented topics such as innovation, digital China, green transformation, rural revitalization, and people's wellbeing.
- "人工智能+" action: useful for discussing new youth capabilities, digital literacy, and technology ethics.

Avoid:

- Pure motivational prose such as "星辰大海" without course concepts.

### 3. 文化自信 / 传统文化传承

Use for: 思想道德与法治, 中国特色社会主义, 近代史纲要.

Core question: how can tradition be transformed into living contemporary practice?

Current examples:

- Fifteenth Five-Year Plan cultural sections and cultural digitalization.
- Current museum,文旅融合,数字文化,中华优秀传统文化创造性转化/创新性发展.
- Red resources and youth education, when linked to concrete education methods.

Avoid:

- "国潮" examples that are already stale unless updated with a current case.
- Treating culture as decoration rather than as value, memory, identity, and practice.

### 4. 科技创新 / 新质生产力

Use for: 中国特色社会主义, 马克思主义基本原理, development-themed essays.

Core question: how do innovation, labor, institutions, and productive forces reshape modernization?

Current examples:

- 2026 "十五五"规划: high-quality development, AI, data resources, strategic emerging industries, future industries.
- 2025 State Council "人工智能+" action: AI integration with technology, industry, consumption, livelihood, governance, and global cooperation.
- Education Power Plan 2024-2035: education-technology-talent integration.

Avoid:

- Listing technological achievements without explaining productive-force logic or institutional support.

### 5. 生态文明 / 绿色发展

Use for: 中国特色社会主义, 马克思主义基本原理, ecology and development essays.

Core question: how can development and protection be unified rather than opposed?

Current examples:

- 2026 "十五五"规划: green transformation and Beautiful China construction.
- 2026 government work report: annual carbon-intensity, green transition, and high-quality development targets.
- "人工智能+" action can support environmental monitoring and precision governance when discussing technology-enabled ecology.

Avoid:

- Only writing "绿水青山就是金山银山" without explaining the mechanism from resource protection to development quality.

### 6. 规则与自由 / 法治意识

Use for: 思想道德与法治, law-related essays.

Core question: how does rule-based order protect meaningful freedom?

Current examples:

- Civil Code and rule-of-law education remain foundational; verify if specific articles are cited.
- 2025 民营经济促进法: good for writing about fair competition, law-based governance, and market order.
- Digital governance and AI governance topics should use latest official documents before making claims.

Avoid:

- Treating "规则" as simple obedience; explain how law protects rights, order, and fairness.

### 7. 开放共赢 / 人类命运共同体

Use for: 中国特色社会主义, contemporary China and world essays.

Core question: how does China's development relate to the world?

Current examples:

- 2026 government work report and "十五五"规划 sections on high-level opening up.
- Global Development Initiative, Global Security Initiative, Global Civilization Initiative, Global Governance Initiative, if official-source verified.

Avoid:

- Empty international-slogan stacking. Use one concrete cooperation mechanism, event, or policy.

### 8. 基层治理 / 形式主义 / 制度建设

Use for: 中国特色社会主义, 思想道德与法治, governance papers.

Core question: how do institutions turn values into effective governance?

Current examples:

- 2025 民营经济促进法 for market governance and fair development.
- 2026 "十五五"规划 for governance modernization and high-level socialist market economy.
- Central or State Council documents must be verified if a specific reform is named.

Avoid:

- Blaming individuals only; use system analysis: rights, responsibilities, resources, incentives, procedure.

## Writing Use

When writing a red-course paper, pick at most two current examples. Explain their logic. A paper that name-drops five policies is usually weaker than a paper that explains one policy well.
